Try It Yourself

You can use this page to make a test transaction. This will allow you to observe the functionality of the SALT platform before signing up for a sandbox account.
  • Click Submit on the form below to create a test transaction. The transaction response will be displayed.
  • Log in to the Merchant Management Console (MMC) by clicking here
  • Click on Payments in the left-hand navigation bar
  • You will be able to view a list of all recent transactions. Clicking on any of these transactions will provide further details, as well as void or refund capabilities.
  • To locate a specific transaction in the MMC, you can use any identifier from the transaction details below in search bar within MMC. Ex: ORDER_ID or TRANSACTION_ID.
